About

Who We Are

Everything we do, we believe in cultivating thriving communities and to fostering good local government.

Preston Drake Management is a highly selective field strategies firm that is committed to securing victories for political candidates and growing the audience for non-profit organizations.

We only provide our services to political candidates and non-profit organizations that have sound reputations, support our communities to be better educated, create environments where businesses can thrive, and championing efforts for our communities to be safer.

What We do

We manage the ground game for your political and advertising campaign. We create effective field strategies to reach your targeted audience through our professional door to door canvassing and phone banking services. Our fierce desire to secure your victory, grow the number of your audience and passion for going above and beyond for your needs, will give you the competitive advantages to win your race and increase the influence of your organization.
