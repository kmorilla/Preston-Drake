About

At Preston Drake Management, we are committed to integrity, consistency, and excellence; these are the values that distinguishes us from others.

We are a selective grassroots advocacy, community organizing, and fundraising firm. At Preston Drake Management, our clients have the option to choose the service or services that is tailored to their campaign needs.

Our network of professional door to door canvassers, phone bankers, fundraisers, and organizers gives our clients the necessary competitive advantage that lead to victory.
